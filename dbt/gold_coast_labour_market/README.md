### Data Caveats:

LGA data includes all Queensland LGAs (prefix 3), not just capital cities. This means remote communities (Palm Island, Yarrabah, Doomadgee, etc.) are included and have very high unemployment rates. Filter by region when comparing Gold Coast to other capital cities.
